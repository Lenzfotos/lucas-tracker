Group feedings into a session
Babies often feed in clusters. You can combine several feedings into one feeding session:
  1. Tap each feeding you want to combine — a checkmark appears.
  2. Select at least 2 feedings.
  3. Tap Group to merge them.
The session shows the total amount eaten and counts as one feeding on your dashboard. Ungroup anytime with the button on a session. Only feedings can be grouped for now.
